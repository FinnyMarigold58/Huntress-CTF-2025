# QRception
**CTF:** Huntress CTF 2025
**Category:** Warmups  
**Author:** John Hammond
**Points:** 10  
**Challenge Description:**

```md
Wow, that's a big QR code! I wonder what it says!
```

Scanning the provided QR will give you ascii art of another qr code.
